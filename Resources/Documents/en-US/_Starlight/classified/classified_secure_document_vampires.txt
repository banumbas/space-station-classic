[bold]Subject:[/bold] Vampire Lords
[bold]Association:[/bold] Vampire Council of Faust
[bold]Objectives:[/bold] Unknown

Reports of 'Vampires' on board Space Stations in the Starlight Sector have been confirmed. Live specimens have been interrogated and revealed the existence of a supposed 'Vampire Council' composed of 'Vampire Lords'.

While clearly deranged, these 'Vampires' do posses powers and abilities from an as of yet unidentified source and should be treated as a serious security risk.

Attempts to contact these Vampire Lords have proven mostly unsuccessful, bar one brief communication demanding the return of their thralls.

We will keep you apprised on developments, in the meantime be on alert.
