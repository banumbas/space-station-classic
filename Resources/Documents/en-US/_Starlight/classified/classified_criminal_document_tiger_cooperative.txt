[bold]Subject:[/bold] Tiger Cooperative
[bold]Association:[/bold] Unknown
[bold]Objectives:[/bold] Unknown

The Tiger Cooperative was formed by Alvin Shawn Martinson, an extremist former Christian from the American Territories of Earth. A crisis of faith following first contact with the Skrell, Martinson concluded that Humanity were not God's chosen people, but that they must be out there somewhere. The quest to find God's chosen people attracted fellow skeptics, which quickly developed a cult around Martinson and grew more and more extreme as they became more insular.

Following several attacks on population centers intended to 'luring out God's True Chosen', the cult was persecuted legally and fled Earth.

Reports of their activity during this time are hazy but it is believed based on accounts from former and current members of the Tiger Cooperative that sometimes roughly 40 years later, the cult came into contact with a Changeling. Martinson may have even been consumed and had his identity assumed at this point, but it is impossible to say.

The cult rapidly morphed their beliefs into Changeling worship, believing them to be God's True Chosen. They began operating out of a secret bunker, the Cathedral of the True Angels. Members dedicate themselves to the Changelings, primarily by capturing sacrifices to satisfy their hunger. To accomplish this, the Cooperative has evolved into a militant organization, conducting violent raids while often enhanced by powerful drugs that push them beyond normal human capabilities.

Martinson himself is believed to still be alive within the compound, his life either prolonged with drugs or from being assimilated by a Changeling.
[/mono]
