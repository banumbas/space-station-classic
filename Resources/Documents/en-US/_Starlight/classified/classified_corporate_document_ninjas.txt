[bold]Subject:[/bold] The Spider Clan
[bold]Association:[/bold] Unknown
[bold]Objectives:[/bold] Theft of Scientific Research, Sabotage

The Spider Clan is one of several Clans of Space Ninjas. While most have kept their operations far more clandestine, several incidents within the Starlight Sector have revealed the Spider Clan's existence to us.

The Space Ninjas have an array of advanced technology including stealth suits, charging gloves, and a katana that allows for controlled short range Bluespcae travel through walls. The source of these devices is currently unknown. Several items have been secured for study.

The primary target of the Spider Clan appears to be our scientific research. It is possible they are being paid by a rival corporation to steal our findings and technologies, and that corporation is in turn supplying them with their equipment. We have no leads on this front however.
[/mono]
